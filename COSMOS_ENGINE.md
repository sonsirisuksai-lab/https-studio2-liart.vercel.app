# COSMOS OS Constitution v1.0

## ARTICLE 1: THE WORLD COMES BEFORE THE INTERFACE
Never design screens first. Design the universe. Every view must belong to a Realm.

## ARTICLE 2: EVERYTHING HAS PURPOSE
No decoration. Every particle and glow must communicate state, history, or relationship.

## ARTICLE 3: PHYSICAL FORM IS MANDATORY
Information is an object. 
- Knowledge = Books/Scrolls
- Projects = Cases/Blueprints
- Memories = Crystals/Spheres
- Tasks = Tokens/Chips

## ARTICLE 4: REALM IS TOTAL TRANSFORMATION
Changing a Realm changes Materials, Physics, Robin's behavior, and Sound.

## ARTICLE 5: ROBIN IS CREW
Robin is a physical entity in the world. Robin moves, carries, repairs, and thinks visually.

## ARTICLE 6: MOTION IS LANGUAGE
Motion teaches hierarchy and physics. Opening a project is "Unfolding", searching is "Gathering Particles".

## ARTICLE 7: NO GENERIC DASHBOARDS
COSMOS is a Command Bridge, not a SaaS app.

## ARTICLE 8: THE WORLD NEVER SLEEPS
Passive motion (Ambient events) ensures the universe feels alive even without user input.
